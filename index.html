Industry 4.0 - Reinforcement Learning Exercise

Dodgy Online Casino

The aim of this game is to sucessfully predict if the number rolled by a dice will be odd or even

With a normal dice, the chance of rolling odd or even is 50/50. However, in this casino the game has been rigged

Your task is to use reinforcement learning to find out how you can increase your odds of winning

To do this you will use a container of 10 red LEDs and 10 green LEDs to make your predictions and to keep track of how successful you have been

How To Play

  1. Make a prediction by closing your eyes and taking an LED from your container

    Red = Even

    Green = Odd

  2. Click "Roll" to roll the dice

  3. If the prediction was correct, put the LED back in the container

    If the prediction was incorrect, put the LED to the side

After you have played 20 times, count how many of each LED is in your container

Using this information determine if you would be more likely to win if you predict odd or even?

Roll the dice a further 20 times predicting which outcome you have found to be most likely. Keep track of how many times you win
